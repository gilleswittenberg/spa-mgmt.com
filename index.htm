Thyme™ / Fish Spa Customer Management Software

A software system specifically designed for the management of Garra Rufa Fish Spas globally

features include:

  • allocation of customer to specific fish spa tanks, hand or foot spa
  • health and safety compliance
  • customer time management efficiency - customers pay for time used after treatment
  • maintenance modules for water testing and routine maintenance reminders
  • online revenue tracking through secure internet address, including smart phone and tablet
  • customer base management, email distribution and newsletter options
  • gift card and promotion sales and tracking
  • group reservations, online reservation tool
  • multiple language options

Thyme Fish Spa Management Software is available now, cost effective and easy to install, only an internet connected computer is required.
